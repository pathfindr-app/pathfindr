How to Play

  1. You'll see a START and END point on the map
  2. Draw what you think is the shortest path between them
  3. Click and drag along the roads to draw your route
  4. Reach the END point to submit your route
  5. Watch the A* algorithm find the optimal path
  6. Your score is based on how close your route is to optimal!
