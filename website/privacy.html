Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Introduction

Pathfindr ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your location to start local map challenges near you. We do not sell location data.
  • Gameplay Data: We collect gameplay events, scores, routes, and challenge results to power leaderboards, player history, and product improvements.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address, authentication provider details, and callsign / display name.
  • Purchase Information: Web payments are handled by Stripe. Native mobile purchases are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play through RevenueCat. We do not store your full payment card details.
  • Advertising and Diagnostics: We may use AdMob / AdSense, attribution, device, and diagnostic data to operate ads, measure performance, and troubleshoot the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the game experience
  • Process payments for premium features
  • Maintain leaderboards and game statistics
  • Restore purchases and premium access across supported platforms
  • Detect abuse, fraud, and service issues
  • Communicate important updates about the service

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Such as Supabase, Stripe, RevenueCat, Google AdMob / AdSense, and platform billing providers to run the game and its purchases
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Account Deletion

If you created a Pathfindr account, you can request deletion directly in the app from your signed-in profile. Deleting your account removes your profile and gameplay data, except where we must retain limited purchase or legal records.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@pathfindr.world to exercise these rights.

Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: hello@pathfindr.world
